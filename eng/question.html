How does Databean differ from other software development companies?

Databean is a software developer that started with the vision of developing solutions that would enable small to medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) to enjoy the effects of business reengineering by adding customized functions to the standardized processes.

The company is relatively young. However, its stakeholders ─ the technology salesmen who had been listening the needs of large and small companies and providing appropriate solutions for some 20 years; the young developers who are recognized by the ICT and the software industries for their numerous papers and patents; and the business partners from the automotive parts industry who are well aware of their needs ─ are passionately united under the motto of providing valuable solutions to the SMEs.

Since we understand that each company has its own special processes, we do not sell a standardized product under our brand name. We do not try to sell the expensive solutions of other developers by persuading our customers that they will improve efficiency if they follow the processes of such solutions. We provide the one and only solution appropriate for a customer by adding the unique processes of the SMEs and the functions that will maintain such processes to the open-source codes that had already been proven in the market. For example, if a company X wants to build its CRM system, company X will have a solution named X CRM, not Databean CRM. Also, even a SME that operates without any specific solution can enjoy the maximum return from an inexpensive investment in customized open-source codes.

The aforementioned vision and goals, the use of open-source codes and customized solutions differentiates Databean from other software developers.

How do I submit a request for development of a solution, and how does the development start?

The first way is to start the development of a solution!

First, you can contact one of Databean’s sales representatives and discuss your requirements.

Please do not hesitate to call us on +82-10-4995-7829. The project will not start just because you called and asked a few questions. The software requirement analyst will listen to your exact requirements and discuss them with you.

The second way is to start the development of a solution!

After you fill out the request form, Databean will call you back within 24 hours or e-mail you an estimate.

You do not have to become a member or log-in. You just need to complete the request form and provide some essential information, such as the function you wish to have, the goal you wish to achieve, the name of the software you had in mind. We will then provide you with our answer via the channel you specified.

How long will it take to develop a solution?

Based on our development process and some real-life examples, it takes eight to 13 weeks once we fully understand your requirements.

The period can be shortened, however, if there is not much need for us to customize the codes.

Although we are small in number of staff, we develop our products by going through the process of general meetings, planning, creating the requirement designs, development, testing, interim reports, and so forth. Thus, we cannot deliver our products simply within a day, a week or a month. Also, the customizing process to materialize the requirements may require a longer development period, depending on the complexity of the requirements.

However, we strive to deliver the best solution by the date when our customers wish to start using it.

Can't you deliver the solution faster?

We hold kick-off meetings with our customers before we start developing.

At the kick-off meeting, we review the timeline requested by the customer, plan the development schedule with the intention to satisfy the customer’s desired launching date, and get the customer’s confirmation of the development schedule.

We may be able to devise ways to shorten the schedule as we proceed with the schedule, but it is unlikely that we can shorten the schedule drastically because the existing schedule has been prepared after a thorough internal review.

However, we will make our best efforts to satisfy the customer’s desired schedule!

Couldn’t you develop any solutions other than CRM, ECM, BI, or accounting solutions?

First, we would like to emphasize that we focus on those four areas as stated in our mission statement and company vision.

We have decided to focus on the four areas of CRM, ECM, BI and accounting because the ordinary SMEs require them as basic solutions but fail to manage them well enough. This does not, however, mean that we cannot develop other solutions.

Our developers do nonetheless possess experience in developing various corporate solutions outside these four areas.

If you require a solution outside these four areas, please feel free to send us your requirements for a discussion.

How do I pay for your services?

Our solutions are provided in the form of cloud solutions, and you will be billed according to the number of users.
The fee for cloud solutions will include all costs, such as the server fee, solution fee, etc.

What does your estimate include?

Our estimate consists of personnel expenses and physical facility (server) expenses.

It is the developers who create solutions, and the quality of solutions may differ depending on the skills of the developers and the development period. Thus, we prepare our estimates by: applying the work skills of our developers and project managers to the remuneration standards for software business under Article 16 of the Enforcement Decree of the Software Industry Promotion Act; and the estimate includes the daily wages of software engineers calculated as prescribed by Article 23 of the Statistics Act.

(Source: 2017 SW Technology, as of February 1, 2017)

How are your solutions maintained?

The customers can determine the scope of maintenance after discussion.

However, when the cloud solution is offered, our staff normally carries out periodical tests and troubleshoots immediately when our customers report inconveniences, bugs, etc.

The troubleshooting can be carried out anytime without any additional cost to you.

However, if you wish to add technologies and customize the solution additionally, further charges may apply depending on the degree of work agreed upon during the discussion.
