SmartBox for DataCenter

The immersion cooling for data centers

SmartBox for data centers - it can cool-down up to 21 U of equipment. Data centers rely on a lot of high-performance equipment, which consumes a lot of electricity, requires a large amount of space and creates a high level of noise. SmartBox for Data Centers is designed and manufactured to help the data centers that operate many servers solve their problems. SmartBox offers numerous advantages over the existing cooling systems, such as less use of electricity, smaller physical footprint, and greater degree of integration, longer maintenance of performance, protection against the causes of failure, and longer period available for troubleshooting when a physical failure occurs. SmartBox is a service that applies the technologies that will overcome the shortfalls of the existing cooling methods ─ appropriate for the achievement of carbon neutrality, a topic that is drawing global attention. It is a service that can have a positive impact on customers’ future.

Major features of SmartBox for Datacenter are:

    ●  Stably cools the servers of up to 50kW capacity
    ●  Achieves PUE 1.1, and can materialize the performance of refrigerant to 100%
    ●  Ensures system stability by duplicating the pumps, etc.
    ●  Modularized and easy to scale-up
    ●  The system can be controlled using the system dashboard
    ●  Can send text messages when the server malfunctions
    ●  Provides a lift for installation or withdrawal of server for a greater convenience to the user
    ●  Compatible with all telecommunication methods (including the optical fiber cable)
    ●  The existing servers can be immersed after a simple preparation

SmartBox for Edge

SmartBox for Edge, for corporate and personal users

The immersion cooling system of Databean can cool down the high-performance servers which emit a large amount of heat, even under some extreme conditions. Persons or companies who use the conventional air-cooling method without a separate server room have to bear heavy burdens when maintaining their servers - noise, dust, electricity bills, and additional cost for the space. SmartBox for Edge offers the server cooling solution that can be personal or corporate purposes, so that the customers can run their servers quietly and safely even in their personal offices. Also, Databean offers a customized service that designs SmartBox to handle the customers’ servers that consume various amount of electricity ─ from 8 to 36 kilowatts. With the custom-designed SmartBox, the customers can efficiently run their equipment consuming smaller amounts of energy and leaving less carbon footprint.

The following customers will need SmartBox for Edge:

    ●  Those who need to quickly process data without any time delay
    ●  Those who need to build a system equivalent to a data center in a short time
    ●  Those who need to build a perfect system that can respond to unexpected accidents
    ●  Those who need to cut costs drastically in all steps of system implementation and maintenance
    ●  Those who need to scale-up the infrastructure flexibly and quickly in accordance with the growth of their company
